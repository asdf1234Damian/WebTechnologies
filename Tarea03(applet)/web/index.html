Permutations and factorial

A permutation of n objects is an arrangement of n distinct object in a row. There are six permutations of three objects {a,b,c}

abc, acb, bac, bca, cab, cba

The properties off of permutations are of great importance in analysis of algorithms, and we will deduce many interesting facts about them later in this book.*
